# 📊 Financial Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Power BI dashboard analyzing financial performance across sales, profit, discounts, and market trends for 2013–2014.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor KPIs, identify trends, and evaluate the impact of discount strategies on profitability.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

* **Executive Overview Dashboard**

  * Total Sales: 119M
  * Total Profit: 16.89M
  * Profit Margin: 14%
  * Average Monthly Sales: 7.42M

* **Sales Trends & Performance**

  * Monthly and quarterly trend analysis
  * Seasonal insights (Q4 peak performance)

* **Discount Impact Analysis**

  * Comparison of discount bands vs sales and profit
  * Identification of high-discount risk areas

* **Profitability and Margin Analysis**

  * Product-level profitability insights
  * Segment contribution analysis

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Q4 sales show significant growth driven by seasonal demand
* Paseo is the top-performing product across revenue and profit
* Government segment contributes the highest revenue share
* High discounts increase sales volume but reduce profit margins
